There once was a yellow monkey named Jeremy. Jeremy was always bullied for being yellow. Others would call him pee monkey. Once Jeremy was minding his own buisness, then he fell off of the tree. He was about to die when a red monkey named Rebecca saved him. Jeremy instantly fell in love. They married each other and had beautiful children. Then something horrible happend. The gorrilas slaughtered the monkey clan. Including Rebecca. Jeremy was furious he wanted revenge. Jeremy killed all of the gorrilas. He became the god of all monkeys. THE END.
